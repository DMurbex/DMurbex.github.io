STRADANOVE
"Seeker" are the people who follow me on my journey, between forgotten places, villas, castles, mental hospitals, and churches. They have no face, because they can be anyone, they can only be passing through or living there forever. This series started in 2016 is my main project, the one I carry out with more determination. All the photos were taken in really abandoned places.
MWA
The photographs of the "night sky" series are a look upwards, they are the search for the perfect landscape to put on an August star sky. Shots were made in Milan, near the new palaces of Gae Aulenti square, in Como, from the lake view of the "Giulio Ravelli" take-off platform and in Emilia-Romagna, between the abandoned "Castello delle Carpinete" and from the top of the imposing "Pietra di Bismantova".
